HARDWOODS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

The short version: Hardwoods collects nothing. Everything you enter in the app stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Hardwoods has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind. The app makes no network requests and sends no data — including team names, rosters, player names, or statistics — to us or to anyone else.

Where your data lives

All scorebook data, player profiles, game records, and season statistics are stored locally on your device only. Deleting the app deletes this data. We have no copy of it and no way to access it.

Sharing

When you use a share feature (stat cards, box scores, PDF reports), the app creates the file on your device and hands it to the iOS share sheet. You choose where it goes — by text, email, or any app you pick. Hardwoods never uploads it anywhere itself.

Children

Hardwoods is designed for recording youth sports statistics by parents, coaches, and scorekeepers. Because the app collects and transmits nothing, no personal information about children (or anyone) is ever gathered by us.

Changes

If a future version of Hardwoods ever changes how data is handled, this policy will be updated here before that version is released.
